Privacy Policy for Level5 Gym

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Level5 Gym ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, username
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, bio, gym membership status
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication

Health & Fitness Data

  • Workout Data: Workout completions, exercise logs, workout templates, scheduled workouts
  • Body Measurements: Weight, weigh-ins, body composition data
  • Personal Records: Strength and fitness achievements (PRs)
  • Fitness Goals: Personal fitness objectives and progress tracking
  • Nutrition Data: Meal logs, macronutrient tracking, nutrition goals

User-Generated Content

  • Photos & Media: Profile pictures, workout photos, motivational content images
  • Messages: Chat messages between members and trainers
  • Posts & Announcements: User-created announcements and motivational content
  • Reactions: Likes and reactions to content within the gym community

Usage Information

  • App Activity: Features used, workout completion times, login history
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Services: Create and manage your gym membership account
  • Track Fitness Progress: Store and display your workouts, weigh-ins, PRs, and goals
  • Facilitate Communication: Enable messaging between members, trainers, and gym owners
  • Community Features: Share workouts, announcements, and motivational content within your gym
  • Personalization: Customize your app experience based on your fitness preferences
  • Support Services: Respond to support tickets and technical issues
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and performance
  • Security: Protect against unauthorized access and maintain data integrity

3. Data Sharing & Visibility

Within Your Gym

Level5 Gym is a single-gym community app. Your data is shared with other members of Level5 Gym as follows:

All Members Can See:

  • Your profile (name, username, profile photo)
  • Your workout completions (for leaderboards and community motivation)
  • Approved announcements and motivational content you post
  • Your reactions to community content

Trainers & Gym Owners Can See:

  • All of the above, plus:
  • Your weigh-ins and body measurements (to provide coaching support)
  • Your fitness goals and personal records
  • Your meal logs and nutrition data (when you seek nutrition guidance)
  • Support tickets you submit

You Can See:

  • Your own complete data
  • Other members' public profiles and workout completions
  • Community content, announcements, and scheduled workouts

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase/Firestore: Cloud database, authentication, and secure data storage (runs on Google Cloud Platform infrastructure)

We DO NOT:

  • Collect payment or financial information directly from users (see note below)
  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes outside Level5 Gym

Note on Subscription Tracking: The App allows gym owners to track member subscription status, payment due dates, and membership tiers for administrative purposes. All actual payment transactions occur outside the App. We do not process, store, or have access to credit card information, bank accounts, or other financial payment methods.

Platform Support

Locked In Technologies (platform creator) has limited access to data for:

  • Emergency technical support and troubleshooting only
  • Resolving critical system issues
  • Platform support staff are bound by strict confidentiality agreements

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Automatic Encryption: All data is automatically encrypted during transmission (SSL/TLS) and at rest on Firebase servers - no action required from you
  • Firebase Security: Cloud Firestore security rules enforce role-based access control
  • Secure Authentication: Firebase Authentication manages your credentials with industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based permissions (Member, Trainer, Owner)
  • Monitoring: Automated security monitoring and logging provided by Google Firebase

However: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Storage & Retention

  • Location: Your data is stored on secure Google Cloud servers in the United States (Firebase/Firestore)
  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Backups: Firebase automatically performs backups as part of their managed service for data integrity and recovery
  • Account Deletion: You can request account deletion at any time by contacting your gym owner

6. Your Rights & Choices

Access Your Data

  • View all your personal information within the app
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting support

Update Your Data

  • Edit your profile, goals, and preferences in-app at any time
  • Correct inaccurate information

Delete Your Data

  • Delete specific entries (workouts, weigh-ins, meal logs, messages)
  • Delete your account directly in the app under Profile â†’ Delete Account
  • Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Staff Permissions: Trainers and gym owners have permission to delete exercises from the exercise library to maintain content quality and accuracy
What Happens When You Delete Your Account:
  • Your profile, workout history, messages, and all personal data are permanently deleted from our system
  • Gym administrators are automatically notified of the account deletion via email for administrative purposes
  • A minimal deletion record (containing only username, email, role, and deletion date) is retained for 90 days for audit and compliance purposes, then automatically deleted
  • You will be immediately logged out and cannot recover your account
  • Any active subscriptions or payment obligations remain your responsibility - contact your gym owner directly

Control Sharing

  • Choose what photos to upload
  • Decide whether to post announcements (subject to trainer approval)
  • Control your participation in community features

Opt-Out Options

  • Disable notifications in device settings
  • Limit photo access in device privacy settings

7. Children's Privacy

Level5 Gym is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Users aged 13-17 require parental/guardian consent to use the app.

8. Photo & Camera Access

We request photo library and camera access for:

  • Profile Pictures: Upload or take a photo for your profile
  • Workout Photos: Document your fitness journey (optional)
  • Motivational Content: Share inspiring images with your gym community

You can deny or revoke this permission in your device settings. The app will function normally, but you won't be able to upload images.

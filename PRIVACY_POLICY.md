## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Locked In Technologies LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Level5 Gym mobile application (the "App").

**Important:**  
This App is a software platform provided to gym members. Your gym has its own privacy practices regarding in-person services, facility operations, and membership management. This Privacy Policy applies only to the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Name, email address, phone number
- Username and password (securely stored via Firebase Authentication)
- Profile photo and bio (optional)

### 1.2 Fitness & Health Data
- Workout logs and completions
- Exercise tracking and personal records
- Body measurements and weigh-ins
- Fitness goals and progress tracking
- Meal logs and nutrition tracking

### 1.3 User-Generated Content
- Photos and images you upload
- Messages sent within the App
- Posts, announcements, and comments
- Reactions to community content

### 1.4 Usage & Device Information
- App features used and activity logs
- Device type, operating system, and app version
- Login timestamps and session data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- **Operate the App:** Provide workout tracking, meal logging, and communication features
- **Enable Community Features:** Display user activity within gym communities
- **Facilitate Communication:** Enable messaging between members and trainers
- **Improve the Platform:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and performance
- **Provide Support:** Respond to technical issues and support requests
- **Maintain Security:** Protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity

---

## 3. Data Sharing

### 3.1 Within Your Gym

The App is designed for gym communities. Data visibility depends on your role and gym configuration:

**Typically visible to other gym members:**
- Profile information (name, username, photo)
- Workout completions (for leaderboards)
- Community posts and announcements

**Typically visible to trainers and gym owners:**
- All of the above, plus:
- Weigh-ins, body measurements, and fitness goals
- Meal logs and nutrition data
- Support requests

Gyms control data access within their community. Contact your gym regarding specific data visibility policies.

### 3.2 Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the App:
- **Google Firebase/Firestore:** Data storage, authentication, and cloud infrastructure
- **Apple Services:** App distribution via the App Store

These providers have their own privacy policies and security practices.

### 3.3 Platform Support

Locked In Technologies LLC (the Company) may access user data for:
- Technical troubleshooting and support
- Platform maintenance and security
- Legal compliance

### 3.4 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information if required by:
- Law, regulation, or legal process
- Governmental or regulatory request
- Court order or subpoena
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety

### 3.5 What We Do NOT Do

We do not:
- Sell personal information to third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing outside the App
- Collect payment or financial information (gyms handle payments separately)
- Share data with unrelated third parties

## 4. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption:** Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Firebase Security:** Cloud Firestore security rules enforce role-based access
- **Secure Authentication:** Firebase Authentication with industry-standard encryption
- **Access Controls:** Strict permission-based access

**Important:** No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Storage & Retention

- **Location:** Data is stored on Google Cloud servers (Firebase) in the United States
- **Retention:** Data is retained while your account is active
- **Account Deletion:** You may delete your account at any time. **Workout and personal data will be deleted within 30 days** of account deletion.
